About Debate Club

The Debate Club is a platform for students to engage in intellectual discussions, develop their critical thinking and public speaking skills, and explore various topics and issues.

Objectives of Debate Club

  • To foster critical thinking, research, and analytical skills among students.
  • To provide a platform for students to express their opinions and engage in respectful discussions.
  • To develop public speaking, communication, and teamwork skills among students.

Activities of Debate Club

  • Organizing debates, discussions, and workshops on various topics and issues.
  • Conducting training sessions and workshops to develop debating and public speaking skills.
  • Participating in inter-school and inter-college debate competitions.
