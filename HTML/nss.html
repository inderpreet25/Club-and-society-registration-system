National Service Scheme (NSS)

About NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a government-sponsored public service program conducted by the Department of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India. The scheme aims to involve undergraduate postgraduate students in various community service activities and national development programs.

Objectives of NSS

  • To develop among students a sense of social responsibility and commitment to national development.
  • To enable students to participate in community service and national development programs.
  • To foster leadership qualities, teamwork, and discipline among students.

Activities of NSS

  • Community service programs, such as blood donation camps, health awareness campaigns, and environmental conservation activities.
  • National development programs, such as disaster relief and rehabilitation, and national integration camps.
  • Leadership training programs and workshops.
