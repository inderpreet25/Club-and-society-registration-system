National Service Scheme(NSS)

About NSS

NSS(National Service Scheme) is a government-sponsered public service program conducted by the Department of Youth Affairs and Sports of the Government of India.

Objectivies of NSS

  • To develop the personality of student through community service.
  • To utilize the knowledge and skills of student for the betterment of socitey

NSS Activities

  • Tree Plantation
  • Blood Donation Camp
  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyan
