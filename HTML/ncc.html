About NCC

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth development movement in India. It provides opportunities for the youth to develop discipline, leadership, and a spirit of adventure and patriotism.

Objectives

  • Develop leadership and character in youth
  • Promote national integration and unity
  • Encourage service to the nation
  • Instill discipline and self-reliance

Activities

  • Drills
  • Training
  • Adventure Activities
